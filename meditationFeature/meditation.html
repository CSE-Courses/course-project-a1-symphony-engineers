Trancendental Meditation

Transcendental Meditation (TM) is a specific form of silent, mantra meditation and the organizations that constitute the Transcendental Meditation movement.Maharishi Mahesh Yogi created and introduced the TM technique and TM movement in India in the mid-1950s.

How to Meditate for Beginners

Most first-time meditators find it strange to sit in silence, to sit with their innermost thoughts and feelings, to sit and do nothing — the very things that, funnily enough, the mind tends to resist. To a beginner, meditation might initially feel a little alien, perhaps even daunting, but that’s okay. People have been meditating for around 3,000 years, and many have doubtless experienced the same reticence, trepidation, or wonder that first-time meditators often feel.

Mindful Meditation

Mindfulness meditation is a mental training practice that teaches you to slow down racing thoughts, let go of negativity, and calm both your mind and body. Mindfulness techniques can vary, but in general, mindfulness meditation involves a breathing practice and awareness of body and mind.
